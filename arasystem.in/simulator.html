Blind Spot Detection
Left & right mirror HC-SR04 sensors with OLED HUDs that flash faster as vehicles approach — 12.5 Hz strobe at 10 cm.
💡
Auto Headlight Dimmer
Algorithm A switches to low beam when oncoming traffic is within 300 cm, restores at 320 cm to prevent flicker.
🎯
Haptic Helmet Ring
10 vibration motors in a 360° spatial layout give the rider directional awareness of obstacles without looking away.
🕳️
Pothole Detection
Algorithm C fuses ultrasonic + IR + IMU: detects road surface drops only when lean angle is stable, ruling out cornering.
📡
Live WiFi Dashboard
ESP2 hosts an async web server at 192.168.4.1. The companion Android app streams GPS & nav waypoints back in real time.
⚡
Dual-MCU Architecture
Safety loops pinned to MCU1, network & haptics on MCU2. Zero-latency 115200 bps UART link with STX/ETX framing.
